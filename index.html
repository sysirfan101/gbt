About us

We are a committed group of IT enthusiasts, problem solvers, and innovators at GreenBuild Tech Inc. (GBT), driven by a passion, for reshaping and empowering the organizations through sustainable and innovative digital IT solutions, ensuring they operate efficiently while minimizing their costs and environmental impact.
